the person behind it

one brain,
blooming slowly

Hi, I'm Nex. I build moonbloom because the tools I needed didn't exist, so I'm making them myself, slowly, for a brain that isn't the expectation.

When I'm not building, I'm probably deep into East Asian pop culture in some form, gaming way later than I should be, or down a rabbit hole about why a language works the way it does. That curiosity is the real special interest, linguistics is just where it shows up most.

moonbloom is the studio I started to build this way. there's more coming, slowly, for people who get it. ✨
